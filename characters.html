IGOR LOMAEV

Age 33. A private and troubled individual whom nobody quite understands. He is extremely superstitious - borderline crazy. His personality could be described as High on complexity, self-reliance and imagination. He is just as much defined by what he’s not, though - he is not dutiful, not warm, not emotional and definitely not gregarious. He doesn’t drink or smoke and, on the whole, doesn’t seem much to like people - which makes his job to kill them a little bit easier.

IGOR LOMAEV

Gravbike Riders

(Actually Grav Bikes is the best name). Grav bikes uses gravity and anti gravity to create levitation and locomotion. It can go upto a height of 120 meters and a speed of 85km/h. Equipped with lasers and plasma the Gravbikes are the present(future) world cavalry. The Gravbike riders are specialised combatant designed to be lightweight and yet effective as a rider. But this doesn’t meant Grav Bike Riders are just a bot with bike driving program. GB Riders can repair and maintain Grav bikes and other machinery. And when the Grav Bike is beyond repair , they can also function as a low grade Militia.

Gravbike Riders

Maddie Nowak

Maddie is all about career - incredibly ambitious and one of the most diligent and devoted soldiers there is. But she is also hugely gregarious. When she stops talking, you know it’s serious. She also scores superhumanly on vigilance and is much more self-reliant than most people. She loves her father, who she says helped her become who she is today. He taught her to hunt white-tail deer in Hamilton County (upstate New York).

Maddie Nowak

Hover Rider

The Militia and Militia+ have one disadvantage that is they are slow infact it is a pathetic speed. Hover riders seems to eliminate this issue. With their low weight and advanced sensors Hover riders can easily give their enemies a hard time, especially in Urban warfare. Hover riders are a good way to quickly cover on the surrounding area in a battle. Given they have less generation time, they are a good choice of a large landscape battlefield. Hovers boards were designed in a very intuitive manner and can be used by humans.

Hover Rider

XU JUN

When you find out later that the robots are protecting civilization, you challenge your rival about the destruction of whole towns. But your rival says the towns were military. There is no civilian-class. You say kids are civilians, but the robot sniper coldly points to their official status when growing up in a military base. Robots see in black and white - these are not civilians.
