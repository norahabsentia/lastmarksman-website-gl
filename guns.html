Meson Zapper

Meson Zapper

The MZ is a type of particle extractor and accelerator that uses energy of mesons to cause damage. Its high damage and lasting ill effects has compelled United Governments to ban this gun for any kind of use on earth. But then who is UG now ?

Zental

Zental

The Zental is a bullpop type assault rifle, as a lot of improvement over its predecessors like the recriprocation problem of the charging handle was removed, abilty to add a scope. The Zental is much more compact and high performance when compared to other bulpops, making it a favourite for combat missions. The Zental found its first use by the UN peace forces in the war on Tzura river against koila koko terrorists. If ever you are loosing an argument about why bullpop rifles are best, use Zental as a trump card, and rest your case.

Growler

Growler

The Growler Covers the sky with bullets when it starts to fire. Growler is a Very heavy general purpose Machine Gun that can work as both anti aircraft and anti material gun. Given its heavy weight mobility is a big concern. Genrally it is mounted on armoured vehicles

Aster Blast

Aster Blast

The Alter Blast is a Designated Marksmen rifle used by PLA and CSF alike. It is not a sniper rifle in a true sense but a designated marksman rifle intended to fire beyond the range of infantry assault rifles. Aster Blast became famous amongst marksmen and sniper during the Sino-Russian war; With both the countries trying to get a piece of Korea, AlterBlast gave PLA and advantage over Russian infantry and helped PLA secure thier posts for a longer duration.

Fusinomer

Fusinomer

Actual core machinery of this gun is less than an inch, rest machinery is to keep the gun and shooter safe and ofcourse some part for shooting and scope.The Gun uses the unparalled power of cold fusion and produces an unparalled beam that destroys any kind of material aimed at. Having said that the gun hasnt been very popular, as its expensive and has a slow fire rate (and for obvious reasons that is shooters safety ..shhh...)

VoltInferno

VoltInferno

Hurling fireshots at the enemy Volt inferno is used for one purpose install fear in the heart of enemy. This results in the early surrender of enemy and make negotiation and terms of surender easy.Volt inferno finds a lot of use when the Enemy is hiding in cover or is inside a bunker. Should you have any doubts give it a shot.

Phosgen

Phosgen

Was designed originally to be used by the Peoples Liberation Army, But after due consideration and its effectiveness it was included as official gun for Special Forces. Lightweight and high accuracy made this gun a favourite in Special Forces as well.

Quitonator

Quitonator

Quitonator is a lightweight, designed on the specification of Precision International standards of 2020. It is a direct impingement gas-operated anti-materiel rifle, meaning it is shorter and effective in the same package. On the design and mechanism side it draws heavily from its british counterpart, but can cover a larger distance with higher accuracy.

Meteoid Gun

Meteoid Gun

While a large number of particle physists were debating the existance of meteons, A german firearms company lanuched the meteoid gun, putting an end to all debates. Meteoid gun borrows heavily from bolt series gun in terms of design. Meteoid Gun sold a record number of pieces in the first three years of invention and militaries were racing to adapt to the gun . Although technically its an assault rifle , it also became popular amongst DM, because of its moderately long range.

BoltGun 2.7

BoltGun 2.7

The 2.7 version was made with only one purpose in mind , increase the damage, and that, it did, at the cost of a low increse in weight, and almost no change in the recoil. BoltGun 2.7 soon replaced 1.2 in terms of market share despite of being expensive and soon became everyones favourite.

Hyperoid

Hyperoid

The Hyperoid belongs to CM series which is indigenously manufactured antimaterial sniper rifle, designed to hit through military equipment. Its ultrahigh accuracy and high damage makes it a deadly rifle but its heavy weight restricts it use for specific combat use only. CSF used Hyperoid against both the militant factions responsible for civil war in Taiwan. Its ability to go through walls chased away militias and forced both the forces to surrender.

Unstroider

Unstroider

Oroginally designed as a gun to be used in space to destroy small asteroids coming towards space station . Good luck or bad, Irony or not Unstroider is considered to be the most lethal guns of all times, and has been frequently used as a weapon rather than a tool.

Anarky

Anarky

Created by an underground organisation without a valid license, the Anarky earned its name and reputation in the dark alleys of Atlanta. Cheap free and easily available Anarky was the first choice of criminals back in the days when this gun was introduced.

Gorreator

Gorreator

God like accuracy, ease of use, lethal damage, and a soundless modus operandi makes this rifle a first choice of carrying out a couvert operaton. Ever since its creation the gun has been popular in spybots and snipers alike.

RedDevil

RedDevil

Considered as vanilla of all guns, RD is one of a long line weapons that has kept the earth safe and helped galactic peace force keep invaders at bay. It's basic yet effective fire power against any kind of armor makes it first choice of a general purpose mode.

QuarkRifle

QuarkRifle

Deriving the power of strangelets, we can proudly say that this is a result of research of over a century; The Quark Rifle is meant to Higgs the shit out of your Boson. This rifle is probably the best rifle that utilises the particle energy to create a high impact damage at the same time be accurate.

BoltSpray

BoltSpray

This gun is mean for creating a havoc and confusion in closed combat. With its ability to go berserk when the trigger is pressed the bolt spray literally sprays bolts over the shot area; meaning there is no escape for the enemy.

Wrandolt

Wrandolt

Carefully designed to hurl energy beam at the target with accuracy, the Wrandolt is often confused as a DM rifle. Lightweight and quick firerate make it an Ideal companion for the assault jobs.

Fusinomer XA

Fusinomer XA

Thanks to the overhaul of the coldfusion technology, using different elements similar effects could be obtained. This led to the development of Fusinomer XA, the gun had a trade off in terms of fire power against fire rate (and safe for shooters, which isnt a bad tradeoff at all).

BoltGun X.A

BoltGun X.A

The latest version of boltgun, that increases greatly upon accuracy and fire rate. The gun was cost effective compared to its predecessors and wiped the market upon reelease. Although its built for assault use, its slightly long range made it a popular one amongst the DMs. UG, American and Indian troops used BG XA heavily during the middleeastern civil war.

Aegroid

Aegroid

Aegroid was the result of flagship project of the Russian Weapon Research Dept called as One Weapon.Lightweight accurate and high performance makes this gun a success. One can understand the popularity of Aergoid by the fact that number of Aegroid used in the world is of what is used in Russian Fedration. It is also available in several barrel lengths to cater to all types of combat need.

Singlon 40 X

Singlon 40 X

The original Singlon 40 was a military type-classified version of the classic Bolt 700; it was man-made, and had a uni-stock of wood. Todays Bolt Action rifles are much sophisticated than that but the action type still remains the same. The Singlon 40 Series is meant for varied sniper use, it works well with and without a bipod/tripod stand and deals high damage with great accuracy, its easy to carry and its very cheap compared to any other sniper rifles.

BoltGun 1.2

BoltGun 1.2

Bolt Gun 1.2 redefined the combat scenario when it was introduced. Delivering quick damage with high speed and accuracy made this gun popular amongst galactic mercenaries and defense personals. Bolt Gun 1.2 was the first approved version to be used by any States's Defence sector that used bolts as ammunition.

Hexaton

Called Let

Hexaton can be considered as the first few laser guns that were made seriously and were taken seriously. It paved a way for the popularity of laser guns. Quick reload , high rate of fire power with maximum accuracy made it the most effective weapon for close combats.

Needle aka RG-14

Needle aka RG-14

This antimaterial sniper rifle was build to penerate through light armoured vehicles and thick walls . But it doesnt hesitate to go through the skull either. Some of its improvements have made it very useful in countersniping.

Singlon 40 ZA

Singlon 40 ZA

The Singlon 40 ZA is the latest of the Singlon 40 series , it is more stable and more accurate compared to its predecessors. It has a simplified design with a longer muzzle meant for just one business hit the target .
